Our Math Academy

Are numbers and problem sums confusing your child? Our enrichment classes will equip your child with the ability to solve math problems through our program which incorporates semantic teachings, cognitive thinking, mathematical concepts, and problem-solving skills. With cognitive processes associated with math, our math tuition classes aim to coach your child in Spatial Representation, Counting & Operations, and Logical Problem-Solving. Our Math enrichment classes closely follow MOE’s Mathematics syllabus and materials are updated yearly.
